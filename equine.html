Equine

The connection shared between a rider and their horse is genuinely unique and heartfelt. I comprehend this deeply, having been immersed in the world of horse riding since the age of 8 and being fortunate enough to have my own horse for over 11 years.

My aim is to capture the enchanting moments of connection between you and your horse, preserving them for eternity. Safety is my utmost concern during our photoshoots, ensuring both you and your equine companion are secure.

With my experience as both an equestrian and a photographer, I'm well-equipped to understand your vision and bring it to life. If you're wishing for dreamy equine photos, please reach out.

Let's create timeless memories together!
